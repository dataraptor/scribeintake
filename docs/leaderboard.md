# Eval leaderboard — ScribeIntake

> **The honest claim (lead with this):** *deterministic safety rules — 0 missed on the frozen
> must-escalate set; end-to-end adversarial recall reported distributionally across N runs;
> schema-valid SOAP 100%; faithfulness and no-diagnosis reported with spread.* The senior signal
> is being able to say **exactly which part is deterministic (gated in code) and which is
> distributional (tracked, never gated)** — never "100% recall, period."

Every number on this page traces to a real artifact and carries a one-command regen. Nothing is
hand-typed without a source. Two groups, two very different epistemics.
